How can I download JioMeet App?
  1. On Mobile Device Go to Play Store/AppStore, download the Android or iOS app.
  2. On Windows Visit Download JioMeet for Windows and download the JioMeet application.
  3. On macOS Visit Download JioMeet for Mac and download the JioMeet application.
  4. On JioFiber STB- Go to JioStore, download the JioMeet app for your TV running on JioFiber STB
What are the recommended system requirements to attend a JioMeet call?
ResourcesSpecifications
Android 5.0 and above with min. 2GB RAM
iOS12.1 and above with min. 1 GB RAM
Mac10.13 and above with min. 2GB RAM
Windows10 and above (64-bit CPU only) and min. 8GB RAM
Google Chrome83 and above
Safari13.1.1 and above
Bandwidth1 Mbps (each for up-stream and down-stream)

The virtual background feature has high performance requirements. Make sure your computer meets the following requirements:

  • CPU: Intel Core i5 4 cores or higher
  • 8G RAM or more
  • 64-bit operating system
  • Browser: Chrome latest version (Mobile browsers are not recommended to enable virtual background)
What is the maximum number of participants I can invite to a call/meeting?

Including the host, you can invite up to 250 participants.

How do I Join/Host a JioMeet Meeting?

You can Join/Host a JioMeet Meeting through:

  1. Mobile- Using JioMeets Android or iOS app
  2. Desktop- Using JioMeets Windows or MAC app
  3. WebApp- Using Safari or Google Chrome Browser
  4. TV- Through JioFiber STBs JioMeet App
What is the maximum time duration for a JioMeet Meeting?

JioMeet meeting can last for a maximum of 23 hours 30 minutes.

How do i experience the best video quality?

The video quality that you can get depends on the device you use and the network you are connected to. Considering you are connected to the wired network with dedicated bandwidth you can experience High definition (720p) video quality.
